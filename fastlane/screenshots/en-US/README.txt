iOS App Store Screenshots

Place your iOS screenshots here organized by device size.

Supported device sizes:
- iPhone 6.9" Display (iPhone 16 Pro Max, 15 Pro Max, 14 Pro Max)
- iPhone 6.7" Display (iPhone 15 Plus, 14 Plus)
- iPhone 6.5" Display (iPhone 11 Pro Max, XS Max)
- iPhone 5.5" Display (iPhone 8 Plus, 7 Plus, 6s Plus)
- iPad Pro (12.9-inch) (3rd gen)
- iPad Pro (12.9-inch) (2nd gen)

Requirements:
- Format: PNG or JPG (PNG recommended)
- No alpha channel
- RGB color space
- Minimum: 1 screenshot per device size
- Maximum: 10 screenshots per device size

Naming convention:
- Use descriptive names like: 01_home_screen.png, 02_scanning.png
- Files are uploaded in alphabetical order

Tips:
- You must provide at least one set of screenshots for the largest device size
- Screenshots should be actual device resolution
- Use Simulator or real devices to capture screenshots
- Consider using fastlane snapshot for automated screenshot generation
